01 Who I am

About me

Senior Computer Science student at UNC Charlotte pursuing a B.S. with a concentration in Software Engineering and an Early Entry M.S. with a concentration in Artificial Intelligence. I am currently completing graduate coursework that counts toward both degrees, with the B.S. expected in May 2027 and the M.S. expected in May 2028.

These days my work sits where full-stack meets AI engineering. I build backends in Python with FastAPI and in Java with Spring Boot, React and TypeScript on the frontend, Docker around it, and a real database underneath. Lately I've gone deep on LLM systems — multi-agent pipelines, retrieval-augmented generation, and vector search. My biggest project is Verdict, a multi-agent equity-research app I built end-to-end: hand it a stock ticker and a team of AI agents pull the SEC filings, recent news, and live financials, then work together to land on a Buy, Hold, or Sell call — with the reasoning behind it. It's the kind of thing I'm proud to actually show people.

Right now I'm building constantly — Tally, more backend work, sharpening what I know. I'm looking for an internship where I can join a real team, ship real things, and grow fast. I pick things up quickly and I care about doing it right.
